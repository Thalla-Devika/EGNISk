"About the college"


JNTUH University College of Engineering Jagtial (JNTUH UCEJ) was established in he year 2007 as a constituent college of Jawaharlal Nehru technological University Hyderabad.The college is recognised by UGC under section 2(f) &12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956.JNTUH UCEJ has received THE ENGINEERING EDUCATORS AWARD rom the University of Bradford, UK and Education Matters for exemplary commitment and impactful positive contribution to the educational sector on 8th February ,2014.The college was accredited by NAAC with A+ in 2022 & the department was accreditedby NBA in 2019 & re-accredited from 2023 until 2025.the college acquired Autonomous status from 2023 to 2033.The college is having well equipped state of art laboratories in all the departments and highly qualified faculty.

"Overview of EEE"


The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering have been exceedingly outshining in the college since its inception in 2007. It has been a team of well experienced dynamic faculty . The faculty of EEE department helps the students attain quality education needed for the next generation. Having a very significant growth ,the Department of EEE presently offers a UG program along with the PG program with specialization "Electrical Power Systems".The aspiration is to emerge as a premier institution in Technical education in providing professional and competent engineers capable of making valuable contributions in engineering and technology.The aspiration is to produce competent electrical engineers in th e field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering who can be capable of providing valuable contributions to power and other electrical sectors.

ABout the Symposium


EGNIS an esteemed national level technical symposium hosted by the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering JNTUHUCEJ, serving as Engineering forum for budding engineers to showcase their innovative ideas and connect with like mindset individuals, offering a unique opportunity to get exposure to emerging technologies and contemporary industry practices. The symposiums comprehensive program is thoughtfully curated to challenge participants technical acumen and enhance their understanding of the intricacies of Electrical Engineering EGNIS includes intense competitions lectures, a variety of informative workshops and many more.
